Stereoscopic 3D

This Technique generates a three dimensional image, based on recording and projecting two different images at the same time, the same way our eyes perceive the world. Media Concepts not only captures footage in stereoscopic 3-D, but also edits, captions, and produces animation and visual effects of high, technical and artistic level
